Our Philosophy

Atmananda means self-bliss; this is the core of our philosophy. We believe that the yoga journey begins with one's self. You will learn how to love yourself first and foremost so that you can then spread the love to your students. This journey begins is about alignment with the mind, body, and soul; this is where self-bliss begins.

Welcome to Atmananda

atma(self)—ananda(bliss) no one has the power to give you bliss; only you can elevate yourself to total self-bliss. “Meditation is what you are. It is our natural state of being. It is that constant silence in the presence of sound. It is that constant stillness in the presence of movement. It is that background in the presence of thoughts that arise from moment to moment. It is the unchangeable in the presence of change. Meditation is an essential practice of yoga in the form of mental hygiene. Think of it as a form of deep rest for the mind while in a conscious state. During meditation, the mind lets go of anxiety and agitation and becomes calm and peaceful.
